Alumni Intakes

The Jordan Media Institute's alumni network is growing every year. By the end of the 2018/2019 academic year, a total of 252 students had graduated with a Master's in Journalism and New Media, awarded by JMI and the University of Jordan. The graduates are spread beyond Jordan's borders and many are currently working with reputable local, regional and international media outlets.
